Michael Richard Pence is an attorney by profession and a politician. He is 50th Governor of Indiana and Republican Party nominee for Vice President of the United States in the 2016 presidential election. Pence graduated form Columbus North High School, earned a Bachelor of Arts in history form Honover College and a Juris Doctor from Indiana University Robert H. McKinney School of Law in Indianapolis, Indiana.

How did he got up here:
2000   Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives
2009   Elected as the Republican Conference Chairman
2016   Elected as Donald Trump's running mate for vice presidential
