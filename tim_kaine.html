Timothy Michael Kaine is an American lawyer by profession. Also as a politician he is serving as the junior United States Senator form Virginia. He was graduated from the University of Missouri, and compleated a law degree from Harvard Law School.After becoming Senate in 2012, he got successful in bringing people together across old lines of party, race or region. In the 113th and 114th Congress, Tim many important legislation for Virginia, such as including a bill to recognize six Virginia Indian tribes, preserve the Commonwealth’s historic Civil War battlegrounds. He also co-hosted a bi bipartisan conference. He became Lieutenant Governor of Virginia in 2002 and was inaugurated as Virginia’s 70th Governor in 2006.
