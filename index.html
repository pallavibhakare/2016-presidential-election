Who Is Running for President?

Hillary Clinton officially accepted the Democratic party's presidential Nomination On July 28. Her running mate, Senator Tim Kaine, was chosen as the nominee for vice president.

On July 26, 2016, Donald J. Trump officially accepted the Republican party's nomination. Indians Governor Mike Pence, was chosen as his running mate.
